EMPORIUM
ABOUT US
    Hi, we are a group of student from River Valley High School's CID4 computing module.

    Survey Emporium is a platform that allows user to trade Google Form surveys with each other for free, hence allowing students to obtain a more comprehensive set of first-hand research data.
    
    The vision of this platform is "By students, for students." A currency called "points" is used to let user post their survey. All service can be accesed for free.
HOW TO USE
    The "Home" page is where you can help other user do their survey, each time you finish a survey, you will gain 1 point.

    You can also enter "Detail" page of a specific survey to view it's information in a clearer format. You can press the "Copy Link" button to share the survey with others.

    You can create an account through "Login" page from the sidebar located at the top right corner, apon registeration, you will receive 6 points to start posting!

    After creating your account, head to "Survey" page to post your own survey, each survey will cost 5 points.
LIMITATIONS
    There still exist some limitations:

    1. Chinese and other Non-latin characters cannot be displayed properly.

    2. Some special characters like " ' \ may cause error if it is in survey description or title.

    3. Only google form is supported currently.

    4. There is very limited support for mobile user.
FAQ
    How do I change my password?
        
        The option can be found in the "Profile" page once you have logged in.

    What if I forget my password?

        You can email us at wang_ting_an@students.edu.sg for support.

    What to do if I see inappropriate surveys being posted?

        You can press the red flag at the right side of each survey to report it to us!

    The website doesn't work on my phone?

    What if my survey is bugged?
